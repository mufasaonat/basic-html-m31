Jotaro Kujo (JoJo)

About Jotaro

Jotaro Kujo is one of the main protagonists in JoJo's Bizarre Adventure: Stardust Crusaders. He also appears in Diamond is Unbreakable, Golden Wind, and Stone Ocean. Jotaro is half Japanese, a quarter American, and a quarter English. He possesses the Stand Star Platinum, a powerful Stand known for its incredible speed, strength, and the ability to stop time.

The Story

In Stardust Crusaders, Jotaro and his allies travel to Egypt to defeat DIO, a powerful vampire and long-time enemy of the Joestar bloodline. DIO stole Jonathan Joestar's body, awakened from centuries of slumber, and now wields the Stand The World, which, like Star Platinum, can stop time.
