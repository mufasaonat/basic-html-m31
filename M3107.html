Background

Zaraki Kenpachi is a powerful and battle-loving Soul Reaper from anime Bleach. He is the 11th Division Captain in the Gotei 13 and is known as immense strength. He took the name "Kenpachi" after defeating the previous on, a title given to the strongest fighter. Despite his violent nature, he has a soft spot for a samll girl named Yachiru, his lieutenant.
